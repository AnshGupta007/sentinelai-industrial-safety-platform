OISD-105: INDUSTRIAL SAFETY GUIDELINES
===========================================

SECTION 3: GAS DETECTION SYSTEMS
3.1 All gas detectors must be installed at potential release points.
3.2 Detectors must be calibrated monthly and tested weekly.
3.3 Non-functional detectors must be replaced within 4 hours.

SECTION 4: GAS EXPOSURE LIMITS
4.1 CO: 25ppm (8-hour TWA), 50ppm (short-term)
4.2 H2S: 5ppm (8-hour TWA), 10ppm (short-term)
4.3 CH4: 10%LEL (warning), 25%LEL (critical)

SECTION 5: EQUIPMENT SAFETY
5.1 Pressure vessels must be monitored continuously.
5.2 Corrosion inspection every 6 months minimum.

SECTION 6: HOT WORK PERMITS
6.1 Gas testing within 30 minutes before hot work.
6.2 Continuous monitoring during hot work operations.
6.3 No hot work if flammable gas exceeds 10% LEL.

SECTION 7: LIFTING EQUIPMENT
7.1 All cranes must be load tested annually.
7.2 Daily pre-use inspection mandatory.

SECTION 8: CONFINED SPACE ENTRY
8.1 Continuous gas monitoring required.
8.2 Mechanical ventilation must be operational.
8.3 Rescue plan must be in place before entry.

SECTION 9: WORK AT HEIGHT
9.1 Fall protection equipment mandatory above 2m.
9.2 Scaffolds inspected weekly.
