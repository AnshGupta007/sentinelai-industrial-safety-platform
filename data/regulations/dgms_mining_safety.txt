DGMS MINING SAFETY TECHNICAL CIRCULARS
=============================================

TECHNICAL CIRCULAR 2/2010: SLOPE STABILITY
All open pit mines must have continuous slope monitoring.
Movement >2mm/day triggers evacuation protocol.

TECHNICAL CIRCULAR 3/2016: BLASTING SAFETY
Blasting exclusion zones based on real-time data.

TECHNICAL CIRCULAR 5/2018: GAS MONITORING
Continuous gas monitoring in underground workings.
CO limit: 25ppm. CH4 limit: 0.75% in return airways.

TECHNICAL CIRCULAR 7/2020: EMERGENCY RESPONSE
All mines must have automated emergency alert system.
Muster points must be clearly marked and tested monthly.
