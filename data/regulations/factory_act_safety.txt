FACTORY ACT 1948 - SAFETY PROVISIONS
========================================

SECTION 21: FENCING OF MACHINERY
Every moving part of machinery must be securely fenced.

SECTION 29: LIFTING MACHINES AND TACKLE
All lifting machines must be tested every 12 months.

SECTION 31: PRESSURE PLANT
All pressure plants must be examined at specified intervals.

SECTION 32: FLOORS AND STAIRS
All floors and stairs must be of sound construction.

SECTION 34: PRECAUTIONS AGAINST DANGEROUS SUBSTANCES
Effective measures to prevent burns from hot substances.

SECTION 35: EXPLOSIVE DUST
Measures to prevent accumulation of explosive dust.

SECTION 36: DANGEROUS FUMES
No entry into confined spaces with dangerous fumes.

SECTION 36A: EXPLOSIVE GAS
Measures to prevent fire/explosion from flammable gas.

SECTION 38: WORKING AT HEIGHT
Safety belts and nets must be provided.
